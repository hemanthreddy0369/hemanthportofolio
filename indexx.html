My Futuristic Projects

🌾 Farm2Home

Farm2Home is my innovative project built for the Viksit Bharat Buildathon 2025 🇮🇳. It connects Indian farmers directly with consumers using IoT and AI integration. The app ensures fair pricing, eliminates middlemen, and promotes digital agriculture. Designed with modern UI and 3D animations, it reflects the vision of “Digital India in Agriculture”. It tracks crop quality, weather predictions, and real-time logistics for better yields. Built using HTML, CSS, JS, and future-ready backend concepts. Farm2Home represents my vision to empower farmers through smart technology. Every element was handcrafted by me to showcase my creativity and innovation. It’s not just an app — it’s a step toward India’s digital farming revolution. A futuristic concept that I plan to expand globally 🌍.
